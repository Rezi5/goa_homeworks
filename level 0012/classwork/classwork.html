car, or an automobile, is a motor vehicle with wheels. Most definitions of cars state that they run primarily on roads, seat one to eight people, have four wheels, and mainly transport people rather than cargo.[1][2] There are around one billion cars in use worldwide.

Low-Level Languages Machine Language (Binary): Directly understood by the computer (0s and 1s). Assembly Language: Uses mnemonics and is specific to computer architecture.

High-Level Languages General-Purpose Languages: Used for a variety of applications (e.g., Python, Java, C++). Domain-Specific Languages: Designed for specific tasks (e.g., SQL for databases, HTML for web structure).
